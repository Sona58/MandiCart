About Us


The word Mandicart comes from the word “Cart” or “Kart” which means to carry the valuables from one place to another. Mandicart is an outcome from the thought “Get Time For Yourself” and the journey began from  2012 by the help of Ansal University Gurgaon.. Mandicart.com is a gradually growing online complete shopping store which provides Grocery, Dairy products, Fruits & Vegetables, Personnel & households for Delhi and other categories like mobile, entertainment, books, kitchenware and health equipments for all over India*on just a  click. We take orders over telephone also.  


Our main motto is to help people to get time for them and use their time in other important routine work such as keeping them refreshed by doing their favourite works for leisure and take time for themselves except running to buy grocery and wasting time in huge traffic and wasting petrol, we help our customers to take out time for themselves by fulfilling their demands at their doorstep with utmost satisfaction and after given time period of 4 hours at their convenient time (for dwarka only) .We procure all basic needs of daily use under one roof. We wish to become a complete online shopping stop for the people and give them an opportunity to utilize their precious time and money in other important aspects of their lives like spending time with their family, following their hobbies, staying fit by going to gym or doing other such activities of their liking.

The increasing hectic schedule in the life  gave us a thought to help people to take out some time for their family, friends, and relief themselves from the run to busy market places and wasting their time in buying the household  and other items. We deliver every product you need for your home, through our wholesome online market by just a click or on a phone call.

Our goal is to make an affordable indulgence of our customers through e-commerce shopping and to provide our customers all the products at their doorstep at lower prices with the assurance to deliver the product after 4 hours at their convenience in Gurgaon  and 2 -7  days for all over India depending upon the region after you placed the order.

Mandicart.com is not only an initiative for complete online shopping but it is a brand name which refers to quality and trust and a promise to deal regularly with all the household needs of our customers and made them feel privileged by saving their time and money which they can make use of in different needs and aspects of their life.

Mandicart.com is a team of energetic and young professionals who are always ready to fulfil your demands with total enthusiasm and willing to get another response from you and to serve your needs in a better way on every visit and always hope a prolonged relation with you.
